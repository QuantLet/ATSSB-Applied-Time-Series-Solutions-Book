Name of Quantlet: ATSSB_SeasonalDynamics

Published in: Applied Time Series Analysis and Forecasting with Python Solutions Book

Description: This Quantlet compares a stationary seasonal AR(1) process (phi=0.2) with a seasonal random walk (phi=1.0) at period 12, visualizing their time series, ACFs, seasonal subseries plots, and concatenated seasonal subseries to illustrate the difference between seasonal stationarity and seasonal unit roots.

Keywords: seasonal AR, seasonal random walk, seasonal unit root, ACF, seasonal subseries, month plot, non-stationarity, time series simulation

Author: Huang Changquan, Alla Petukhina, Ruting Wang, Raul Cristian Bag
