Name of Quantlet:    'ATSSB_RandomWalk'

Published in:        'Applied Time Series Analysis and Forecasting with Python Solutions Book'

Description:         'This Quantlet demonstrates that a random walk possesses a stochastic trend by simulating random walk paths, verifying that the variance grows linearly with time via Monte Carlo, and showing that the first differences are white noise through ACF analysis.'

Keywords:            'random walk, stochastic trend, Monte Carlo simulation, variance growth, first difference, white noise, ACF, non-stationarity'

Author:         Huang Changquan, Alla Petukhina, Ruting Wang, Raul Cristian Bag
